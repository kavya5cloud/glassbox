# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Glassbox community a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, identity, or background.

Our goal is to build an open-source project where people feel comfortable learning, contributing, and collaborating.

---

## Expected Behavior

Please:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intentions.
- Welcome newcomers and help them learn.
- Give thoughtful and actionable feedback.
- Focus on improving the project rather than winning arguments.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks or insults.
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that creates an unsafe or hostile environment.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a private discussion or contact the maintainers.

All reports will be reviewed fairly and handled confidentially whenever possible.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct and may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors whose behavior violates these guidelines.

---

Glassbox is built to help developers. Let's make the community just as helpful.
